About Us

Acadia Park Equipment was founded in 1993 by an avid outdoorsman named Frank Longmire. Frank has always enjoyed hiking, fishing, rock climbing, and just about anything else that would permit him to be outdoors. When Frank realized that he found himself traveling all over to find the gear he needed for his excursions he assumed others were having the same issues as well so he started his own business to help other advernturers!
